Review-Based Product Assessment

Leveraging Roberta for aspect-based sentiment analysis to drive actionable product insights from large-scale customer feedback.

Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing large-scale customer reviews to extract actionable insights and identify product strengths and weaknesses through advanced NLP techniques. By processing unstructured text data, we transform noise into clear strategic value.

Problem Statement

The challenge was to accurately classify sentiment and distinct aspects from unstructured text data, which is often noisy and domain-specific, to understand real-time product performance without manual review.

Methodologies & Tech Stack

Roberta Model: Fine-tuned for sentiment & aspect-based classification.
GenAI Integration: Summarization & Contextual Insight Generation.
Processing: Data Preprocessing (Cleaning & Tokenization), Python, PyTorch.
