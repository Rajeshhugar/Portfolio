Review-Based Product Assessment

Scalable aspect-level sentiment analysis and theme extraction from e-commerce reviews to surface actionable product insights.

Project Overview

We aggregate customer reviews from major e-commerce platforms (Amazon, Flipkart, Walmart and other regional marketplaces depending on location), normalize and clean the text, and run an aspect-based sentiment pipeline powered by a RoBERTa model fine-tuned on in-domain e-commerce data. The system extracts aspect-level sentiment scores, clusters recurring themes, tracks sentiment trends over time, and generates concise, actionable summaries and alerts for product and CX teams.

Problem Statement

E-commerce reviews are noisy, multilingual and biased by region and product category. The goal is to reliably identify product aspects (e.g., battery, packaging, fit), determine fine-grained sentiment for each aspect, and surface high-impact themes without manual tagging â€” at scale.

Methodologies & Tech Stack

Data Ingestion: Region-aware crawlers and marketplace APIs (rate-limited, respecting robots.txt and ToS), S3/Blob storage for raw reviews.
Preprocessing: Deduplication, language detection, normalization, spell-correction, tokenization and data augmentation.
Modeling: RoBERTa fine-tuning for aspect extraction and sentiment classification (PyTorch / Hugging Face).
Post-processing: Theme clustering, time-series sentiment aggregation, alerting and generative summaries via an LLM layer.
