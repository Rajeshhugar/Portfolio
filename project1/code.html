Review-Based Product Assessment (GenAI Project)

Leveraging GenAI and Roberta for aspect-based sentiment analysis to drive product insights.

Project Overview:

This project focuses on analyzing large-scale customer reviews to extract actionable insights and identify product strengths and weaknesses through advanced NLP techniques.

Problem Statement:

The challenge was to accurately classify sentiment and distinct aspects from unstructured text data, which is often noisy and domain-specific, to understand real-time product performance.

Methodologies & Tech Stack:

Roberta Model (Fine-tuned for sentiment & aspect-based classification),

GenAI Integration (Summarization & Insight Generation),

Data Preprocessing (Cleaning & Tokenization), Python, PyTorch, Transformers, Flask.
