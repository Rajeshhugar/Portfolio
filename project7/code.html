report_problem The Problem

The auto insurance industry faces significant financial losses due to fraudulent claims, which also cause processing delays for legitimate ones. Manually reviewing each claim is time-consuming, costly, and prone to human error, leading to high operational overhead and a poor customer experience. There's a critical need for an automated, data-driven approach to efficiently and accurately identify suspicious claims.

emoji_objects The Solution

I developed a real-time claims management system that leverages a Random Forest machine learning model to predict the likelihood of fraud. The system analyzes various data points from a claim in real-time, providing an immediate risk score. This allows claim adjusters to prioritize high-risk claims for manual review, while fast-tracking low-risk ones, significantly improving efficiency and accuracy.
